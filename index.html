Artists of the Month

Jungle

Jungle is an electronic music band from London, England known for their feel-good vibe and melodic choruses. Some of their songs include 'Keep Moving', 'The Heat', 'Pray', 'Drops', and 'Time'.

DARKSIDE

DARKSIDE is a musical duo based out of New York City comprised of Nicolas Jaar and Dave Harrington. Known for their experimental and psychedelic style, this duo seamlessly blends electronic production and instrumentalism for a unique sound. Their songs 'The Limit', 'A1', 'Paper Trails', 'Freak, Go Home', and 'Liberty Bell' will give you a good sense of what their about.

Rhye

Rhye is a Canadian musical group led by vocalist Mike Molish. Known for his 'angelic' voice, Molish makes great use of instrumental talent to provide a downtempo, alternative R&B, funk, and jazzy vibe. All of their albums are worth a listen but some of Rhye's best songs in my opinion are 'Taste', 'Count to Five', 'The Fall', 'Hold You Down', 'Stay Safe', 'My Heart Bleeds', and 'Holy'.

Bob Moses

The electronic duo Bob Moses is made up of Jim Vallance and Tom Howie. Based out of New York City, they come together to form a beautiful mix of electronica, deep-house, and indie rock.

Kerala Dust

This London based music group has been around since 2016 and has made their mark in the global electronic scene. Mixing blues, psychedlic rock, and techno into one, this trio of artists delivers a perfect blend of traditional instrumentalism and modern rhythm.
